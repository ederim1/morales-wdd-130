Overview

Purpose

The purpose of this web site is to inform the prospective customer of purchase options, to let them know about rental and instruction packages as well as give them information on how to get to the physical site. It also has the purpose of providing information on how to contact us as well as to take you to visit our social networks and enjoy an image gallery to motivate the purchase.

Audience

The market niche of our business is the closest thing to the niche of people we want to attract to our site. We want to be visited by those who are looking for information to make reservations and learn about things to do and extreme sports in the Yellowstone area. Age range between 18-40 years old, with purchasing power, that want to make a reservation or look for information to get to the site. They would access to our website on their mobile since we will redirect them from other platforms like social media apps.
