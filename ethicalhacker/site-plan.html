    Purpose

    You probably want to get into the world of hacking, even if you are only an apprentice yet. You want to learn and want to know where to start. I want to help newbies to reach their full potential by letting them know the steps, and the paths, and giving them some sites where they can get trained to become professionals. I want the website to present paths and options for people who want to change their path or field, get a better job, and get news and fresh info. The site will give them suggestions and articles to read and get started.

    Audience

    People interested in starting a hacking career

    Introduction Everybody is invited to visit the website. This site is made to help people to get an idea of what cybersecurity is and how it works, but more importantly to find helpful information to be prepared and informed about the threats that are happing out there. People will be able to find where to start in the field of cybersecurity. What path can you take, and which websites can you go to for news and training to get news and articles about it and to find training resources. What would you do here? On this website you would be able to spend your time reading News related to cyber security and related to technology, you will be able to explore paths and careers that you can use to boost your current position or to start in this amazing cybersecurity world. You will only need to share what is career background, as well as what path you would be interested in. Why would you want to visit the site?
